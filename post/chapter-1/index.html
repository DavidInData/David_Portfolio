EDA Lead-Time Analysis

January 1, 2022

  • Does distance have a factor on lead-time (On-Time Delivery)?

  • Performed EDA on anonymized supplier receipt data

  • Parts that were shipped within 100 miles were late on average of 1 day

  • Parts more than 300 miles averaged 2.7 days

  • Parts between 100-300 miles averaged 3.5 days
